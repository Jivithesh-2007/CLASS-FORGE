# Mentor Collaboration - Quick Reference Guide

## For Students

### What Happens When You Submit an Idea?
1. Your idea appears in your dashboard
2. All teachers/mentors see your idea
3. Teachers/mentors can show interest

### What Does "Mentor Interested" Mean?
- A mentor likes your idea and wants to discuss it
- You'll receive an email notification
- The mentor will arrange a Google Meet to discuss

### How to Join the Meeting?
1. Check your email for the meeting link
2. Click the "Join Google Meet" button in the email
3. Or copy the link and paste it in your browser
4. Join a few minutes early to test audio/video

### What Happens After the Meeting?
- The mentor will review your idea
- If they approve it, you'll get an "Accepted" email
- Your idea status changes to "Accepted"

---

## For Teachers/Mentors

### How to Show Interest in an Idea?
1. Go to your Teacher/Admin Dashboard
2. Find the idea you like in "Recent Ideas"
3. Click the "Show Interest" button
4. Student receives notification + email

### How to Arrange a Meeting?
1. After showing interest, click "Arrange Meeting"
2. Click "Open Google Meet" to create a new meeting
3. Copy the meeting link from Google Meet
4. Paste it in the modal and click "Send Meeting Link"
5. Student receives email with meeting link

### How to Approve an Idea?
1. After the meeting discussion
2. Click the "Accept" button
3. Student receives "Accepted" email
4. Idea status changes to "Accepted"

## Email Notifications

### You'll Receive Emails For:

**Students Receive:**
1. ✉️ Mentor Interest Email
   - When: Mentor shows interest
   - Contains: Mentor name, what's next

2. ✉️ Meeting Link Email
   - When: Mentor shares Google Meet link
   - Contains: Clickable meeting link, reminders

3. ✉️ Acceptance Email
   - When: Mentor approves your idea
   - Contains: Congratulations, next steps

**Teachers/Mentors Receive:**
- Notifications when students submit ideas
- Notifications when students join meetings

## Common Questions

**Q: Can multiple mentors show interest in the same idea?**
A: Yes! Multiple mentors can show interest. The student can discuss with any of them.

**Q: What if I don't want to meet with a mentor?**
A: You can withdraw your interest by clicking "Withdraw Interest" button.

**Q: Can I change the meeting time?**
A: Yes, you can create a new Google Meet and share the new link.

**Q: What if I miss the meeting?**
A: Contact the mentor to reschedule. You can arrange another meeting.

**Q: Will my idea be approved if I don't attend the meeting?**
A: No, the mentor needs to discuss your idea before approving it.
